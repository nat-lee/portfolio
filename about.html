About

I have always been infatuated by art in all its forms for as long as I can remember, but have had an absolute passion for illustration and typography since university. I hold a Bachelor in Graphic design and a Diploma in Digital Media, and love turning a static piece of work into interactive art.

Working as a UX designer and developer (some say a deseloper) at Gladeye, an independent digital agency in Auckland, New Zealand I have worked with clients of all sizes both international and local. I have created everything from email templates to fully customised content managed sites in the three years I have been there.

My passion lies in creating interesting and beautiful online experiences that capture users emotions and attention, at Gladeye I had the privilege of being part of a creative and award winning team. Awards we received included New Zealand's Best Design, The Awwwards, The CSS Design Awards and The Communicator Awards.

Skills

I am well versed in Adobe creative suite, but work mostly with Photoshop and Illustrator when designing. While my skill range leads more towards design and front end styling, I frequently work across Javascript, jQuery and Php. I enjoy learning new techniques and software, and am always on the lookout for the latest and greatest jQuery plugin or CSS3 technique to enhance my designs.

Awards

I was privileged to be involved in projects that won some awards. These awards were won by the agency I worked with back home in New Zealand.
