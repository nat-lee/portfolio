Rodney Wayne

Design / Html / Css / Email Template

Rodney Wayne is New Zealand's number one hair salon brand, they needed a new email template that could be content managed in Mail Chimp.

I designed and built this email template so that all the content such as the text and images were easily editable by the client. Panels in the design could be easily re-arranged, added or deleted to giving them the ability to customise it. We wanted to give the client a template that could be easily manageable, but could also versatile enough to create various layouts for each email campaign.
