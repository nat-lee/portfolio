Ecoya

UX Design | Html | Css

Ecoya set out to raise money for the Fresh Tracks Foundation, a charity that aims to improve the quality of life of young people suffering from brain injuries. Dan Carter and Adam Ashley Cooper (New Zealand and Australian Rugby players) were chosen to feature their prized assets (Dan's leg and Adams arm) into limited edition life sized wax sculptures and were auctioned for the cause.

I was responsible for designing and building the promotional two page section on the old Ecoya website, bringing a feminine touch to the concept of Rugby and applying the Ecoya brand to an ever so manly sport.
