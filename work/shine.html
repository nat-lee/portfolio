Shine

Html | Css | Javascript

Shine's safe@home appeal came to us with a goal to help prevent domestic violence in homes. They wanted to raise money to upgraded security in homes that are at a high risk of domestic abuse. We came up with the idea of user's being able to buy time for potential victims, the more money donated the more time potential victims had to keep safe. As you move the slider from left to right the image of the house changes from a dark and dreary looking place to an environment that looks safe and warm. I was in control of the build of this Facebook app, from the styling right through to the final donation form.
